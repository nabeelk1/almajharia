About Al Majharia

Al Majharia is a leading provider of laboratory supplies and modeling services. Our focus is on delivering innovative solutions tailored to the specific needs of hospitals, industrial laboratory and research facilities. With a dedicated team and strategic partnerships, we offer comprehensive services, including lab chemicals, microbiological media, lab disposables, glass-wear, plastic-wear, laboratory design, equipment procurement, installation, supplies and ongoing support. Our goal is to enhance workflow efficiency and drive advancements in medical, research and industrial laboratories.
